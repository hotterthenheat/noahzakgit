This Privacy Policy explains how [LEGAL ENTITY NAME] ("we", "us") collects, uses, and

## 1. Information we collect
- **Account info:** name, email, username, and (if you set one) profile/cover image.
- **Authentication data:** a hashed password (we never store plaintext passwords) and,
  if enabled, two-factor authentication settings. Session cookies are signed (HMAC).
- **Billing info:** subscription tier and status, and a payment-processor customer ID.
  **Card details are collected and stored by Stripe, not by us** — we never see or store
  your full card number.
- **Usage data:** preferences, workspace layout, referral codes, and basic technical
  data such as IP address and user agent (used for session security / single-session
  enforcement).
- **Market/app data** you interact with is not personal data.

## 2. How we use information
To provide and secure the Service; authenticate you and manage sessions; process
subscriptions and referrals; personalize your workspace; communicate with you (e.g.
account, billing, or access emails); and to operate, debug, and improve the Service.

## 3. Legal bases (where applicable, e.g. GDPR)
Performance of our contract with you (providing the Service), our legitimate interests
(security, improvement), your consent (where required), and legal obligations.

## 4. How we share information
We do **not** sell your personal information. We share it only with:
- **Service providers / processors** that run the Service on our behalf — e.g. **Stripe**
  (payments), our **hosting** provider, **market-data** providers, and email delivery —
  under contracts limiting their use of the data.
- **Legal/compliance** recipients when required by law or to protect rights and safety.
- A **successor** in the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale.

## 5. Cookies
We use a signed, HTTP-only session cookie to keep you logged in and to protect your
account. Disabling it will prevent you from signing in.

## 6. Data retention
